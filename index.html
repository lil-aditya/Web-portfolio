ii. About & Philosophy

first principles.

I'm Aditya Gaurav—an undergraduate researcher and engineer at IIT Jodhpur studying Artificial Intelligence and Data Science. I believe the hardest technical problems dissolve when you teach them clearly enough.

My work spans deep learning, computer vision, and distributed systems. I don't just build software; I decompose complex systems into communicable ideas. Whether it's explaining how a convolutional kernel detects edges or why a microservice needs its own failure domain, I approach engineering as an act of translation—turning complexity into understanding.

Currently, I'm conducting research at Nazarbayev University on thermodynamic irreversibility operators for deepfake detection, and at McGill University validating cloud experimentation platforms and programming language specifications.
